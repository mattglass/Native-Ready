# Bootstrap Questionnaire

Ask only the most important missing questions:

1. What is the app called, and what is its primary purpose?
2. What platform are we building first?
3. What are the first 3–5 must-have features?
4. Do you already have design references, Stitch projects, or example apps?
5. Will the app need backend endpoints or cloud data flows?
6. Is Stitch required, preferred, or not in scope for this bootstrap?
7. Do you have a final bundle identifier, or should bootstrap use an explicitly provisional simulator identifier?

Optional follow-up questions:
- What visual tone should the app follow?
- What existing codebase or starter should be reused?
- What matters most right now: prototype speed, internal testing, or production readiness?
- Are Codex, Xcode/Simulator, XcodeBuildMCP, and any required Stitch account/MCP connection ready?
